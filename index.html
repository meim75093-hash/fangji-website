关于我们 About Us

我们专业从事粉防己的种植、加工和销售，拥有多年的中药材种植经验。我们的种植基地位于浙江和江西两地，严格按照 GAP（良好农业规范）标准进行管理，确保每一批产品都达到最高质量标准。 We specialize in the cultivation, processing, and sales of Stephania tetrandra, with years of experience in traditional Chinese medicine cultivation. Our cultivation bases are located in Zhejiang and Jiangxi provinces, managed strictly according to GAP (Good Agricultural Practice) standards to ensure every batch reaches the highest quality standards.

从种植、收获到加工、包装，每一个环节我们都严格把控，确保产品质量的稳定性和可追溯性。我们致力于为国内外客户提供优质、安全、可靠的中药材原材料。 From cultivation and harvesting to processing and packaging, we strictly control every step to ensure product quality stability and traceability. We are committed to providing domestic and international customers with high-quality, safe, and reliable traditional Chinese medicine raw materials.
