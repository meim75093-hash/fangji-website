About Fangji Root

Used for Thousands of Years

馃摐 Historical Origins

Fangji Root, scientifically named Stephania tetrandra S. Moore, is the dried root of the Menispermaceae plant. First recorded in 'Shennong Ben Cao Jing', it has been documented in鍘嗕唬 herbal texts and is a commonly used herb in TCM for dispelling wind-dampness and relieving pain. Mainly produced in Zhejiang, Anhui, and Jiangxi, with Zhejiang producing the best quality.

馃尶 Properties & Meridians

Properties: Bitter, acrid, cold
Meridians: Bladder, Lung
Functions: Dispels wind and relieves pain, promotes diuresis and reduces swelling
Indications: Rheumatic pain, joint swelling, edema, difficult urination

馃敩 Modern Research

Modern pharmacological research shows that Fangji Root mainly contains alkaloids such as tetrandrine and fangchinoline, with anti-inflammatory, analgesic, immunomodulatory, and anti-tumor effects. Tetrandrine is its main active ingredient and has been widely used in clinical treatment.

鈿狅笍 Usage Precautions

1. This product is bitter and cold, use with caution for those with weak spleen and stomach
2. Pregnant women, nursing mothers, and children should use under medical guidance
3. Not suitable for long-term large-dose consumption
4. If taking other medications, consult a physician or pharmacist before use
5. This is a wellness product and cannot replace medical treatment
